About Us

Miraceuticals is a health-tech startup founded by Pharmacist Miracle Ojedayo, dedicated to solving one of Nigeria’s most pressing healthcare challenges: access to safe, affordable, and authentic medicines.

Every year, counterfeit drugs and unreported adverse drug reactions (ADRs) claim thousands of lives across Africa. At Miraceuticals, we believe no Nigerian should lose their life because they couldn’t access genuine medicines or because their drug reaction went unnoticed.

Our Mission

To make safe medicines accessible to every Nigerian home while empowering patients to actively participate in drug safety.

Our Vision

Safe medicines for every Nigerian home.

What We Do

  • Medicine Distribution: We partner with licensed pharmacies to supply only authentic, quality medicines.
  • Fast Delivery: Through trusted courier services, we ensure medicines reach patients quickly and safely.
  • Patient Feedback: Our system allows patients to share experiences and improve healthcare accountability.

Our Core Values

Integrity: We put patients first and maintain the highest standards of trust.
Patient Safety: Every decision we make protects lives.
Innovation: We use technology to solve critical healthcare challenges.
Community Impact: Beyond profit, we aim to save lives and create jobs.

Why Choose Us?

  • Only licensed pharmacies as partners.
  • Medicines that are authentic and affordable.
  • A system that strengthens NAFDAC’s role in drug safety.
  • Job creation in logistics, pharmacy, and technology.

Our Impact

  • Lives Saved: By reducing counterfeit drug use and unreported ADRs.
  • Jobs Created: For pharmacists, delivery riders, tech developers, and support staff.
  • Stronger Healthcare System: By improving drug surveillance and reporting.
  • SDG Contribution: Advancing SDG 3 (Health), SDG 8 (Decent Work), and SDG 9 (Innovation).
